This website holds collection of Latin dictionaries. List of the available wordbooks is in the particular section.

Dictionaries are encoded for desktop computers and mobile devices. Formats and supported dictionary shells are explained in the documentation.

I am open for cooperation. If you want to share your dictionary, join to a digitalization project, or have technical questions, feel free to contact me.
